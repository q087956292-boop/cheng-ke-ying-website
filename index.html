RETURN HOME / 歸途

Every return begins with disappearance.

《歸途》是一系列關於生死、光、記憶與存在的創作。

受到《心經》與《金剛經》的啟發，我試圖透過光與消逝的意象，探索人在無常之中的存在狀態。

作品並不試圖給出答案，而是邀請觀者進入觀看與思索之中，在光與陰影之間重新凝視生命。

ABOUT

CHENG, KE-YING

Born 1997
Kaohsiung, Taiwan

My practice begins with a contemplation of life and death.

Inspired by the Heart Sutra and Diamond Sutra, I explore light as a metaphor for existence, memory, and impermanence.

Through drawing, ink, charcoal, and mixed media, I seek the traces that remain before disappearance.

Rather than providing answers, my work invites viewers into a space of reflection on time, mortality, and the nature of being.
