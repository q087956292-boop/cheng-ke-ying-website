ARCHIVE / 歸途

《歸途》 explores the relationship between death, light, memory, and spiritual existence.

Inspired by Buddhist contemplation, existential philosophy, and poetic silence, the work investigates what remains after disappearance.

Rather than portraying darkness as destruction, the work approaches it as a threshold — where light becomes visible for the first time.

ABOUT

CHENG, KE-YING is a Taiwanese contemporary visual artist exploring the intersection between light, death, memory, and spiritual existence.

Through cinematic imagery, minimal visual language, and philosophical research, the work investigates the invisible emotional structures hidden beneath ordinary reality.

The practice exists between disappearance and illumination — where silence itself becomes visible.
